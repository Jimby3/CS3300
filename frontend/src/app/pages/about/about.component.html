About TaskTrackr

TaskTrackr is a platform created by a group of students to help others manage assignments, projects, and deadlines effectively. Whether you're working on projects or tracking tasks, our app simplifies the process.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide students with an efficient, user-friendly tool for managing their workload. We aim to improve productivity and keep you organized, so you can focus on learning.
