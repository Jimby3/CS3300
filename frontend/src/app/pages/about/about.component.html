About TaskTrackr

TaskTrackr is a platform created by a group of students at the University of Colorado, Colorado Springs to help others manage assignments, projects, and deadlines effectively. Whether you're working on projects or tracking tasks, our app simplifies the process. TaskTrackr originated as a semester project for CS 3300: Introduction to Advanced Software Engineering, taught by Dr. Armin Moin in the Fall of 2024.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide students with an efficient, user-friendly tool for managing their workload. We aim to improve productivity and keep you organized, so you can focus on learning.
